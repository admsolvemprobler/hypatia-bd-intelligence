## SECTION 3: SCOPE OF WORK

### 3.1 Executive Summary
DoIT seeks staff augmentation services for enterprise application development and support across multiple state agency systems. The vendor will provide on-demand skilled IT professionals in roles spanning software development, business analysis, project management, and quality assurance. This is an IDIQ vehicle with task-order based delivery over a base year with four option years.

### 3.2 Detailed Scope
- Provide qualified IT professionals across 8+ labor categories on a time-and-materials basis
- Labor categories include: Software Developer (Java/.NET), Business Analyst, Project Manager, QA Engineer, Database Administrator, Cloud Architect, Cybersecurity Analyst, Help Desk Tier 2
- Resources deployed to DoIT and assigned state agencies across Maryland
- Minimum qualifications per labor category specified in RFP
- Contractor provides benefits, payroll, compliance
- Background investigations required (CJIS-eligible)
- On-site presence at DoIT Annapolis/Baltimore required for designated roles
